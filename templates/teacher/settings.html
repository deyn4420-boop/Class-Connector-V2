🔑 How to get a Gmail App Password
  1. Go to your Google Account → myaccount.google.com
  2. Click Security in the left menu
  3. Enable 2-Step Verification if not already on
  4. Search for "App Passwords" in the search bar
  5. Create a new app password → select Mail
  6. Copy the 16-character password and paste it above
⚠️ Use the App Password (not your regular Gmail password). Your credentials are stored locally in the database.
